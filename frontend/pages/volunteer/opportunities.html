Volunteer Opportunities

Make a difference in the lives of pets waiting for their forever homes.

Become a Volunteer
Animal Care

Help with daily care activities including feeding, walking, grooming, and socializing with our pets.

  • Flexible hours
  • Weekday & weekend shifts
  • No experience needed
Event Support

Assist with adoption events, fundraisers, and community outreach programs.

  • Weekend events
  • Various locations
  • Great for social butterflies
Administrative

Support our office with data entry, phone calls, and general administrative tasks.

  • Business hours
  • Computer skills needed
  • Phone support
Photography

Take photos of our pets for the website and social media to help them find homes.

  • Own equipment required
  • Photography experience
  • Flexible schedule
Social Media

Help manage our social media presence and create engaging content.

  • Remote work possible
  • Content creation
  • Social media savvy
Transport

Help transport pets to vet appointments or adoption events.

  • Valid driver's license
  • Own vehicle required
  • On-call availability

General Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Commit to at least 4 hours per month
  • Attend volunteer orientation
  • Complete background check
  • Sign volunteer agreement
