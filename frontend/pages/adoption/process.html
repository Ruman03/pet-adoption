Adoption Process

Follow these steps to give a loving home to your new companion

1. Browse Pets

Look through our available pets and find your perfect match based on your lifestyle and preferences.

2. Submit Application

Fill out our adoption application form with your information and references.

3. Home Check

We'll schedule a visit to ensure your home is ready for your new pet.

4. Welcome Home

Complete the adoption process and welcome your new family member home!

Adoption Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old with valid ID
  • Proof of residence (lease agreement or mortgage statement)
  • Consent from all household members
  • References from veterinarian (if you have other pets)
  • Adoption fee payment

Frequently Asked Questions

The adoption process typically takes 1-2 weeks, depending on the completion of home checks and reference verifications.

Adoption fees vary by animal type and age. They typically range from $50-$300 and include vaccinations, microchipping, and spaying/neutering.

We offer a 2-week trial period. If the pet isn't a good fit, you can return them and receive a refund of the adoption fee.
